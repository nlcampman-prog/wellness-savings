## What is a Touchstone Essentials Brand Ambassador?

A Brand Ambassador for Touchstone Essentials is someone who shares the brand's mission of "The Good Inside" with their community. Unlike traditional affiliate programs that only pay a small percentage for a one-time click, the Touchstone model is designed for long-term partnership and recurring revenue.

Whether you are a social media influencer, a health coach, or simply someone who loves their zeolite detox and greens, the program allows you to earn by recommending products you already use.

## The Payouts: How Much Can You Earn?

One of the main reasons the Touchstone Essentials program stands out in 2026 is its generous commission structure.

### 1. Retail Commissions
Ambassadors can earn up to **25% on retail sales**. This is significantly higher than many major supplement retailers, which often cap affiliate payouts at 5-10%.

### 2. Recurring Income
Because Touchstone Essentials focuses on daily foundational health (greens, protein, detox), customers often sign up for monthly subscriptions. As an Ambassador, you earn a commission on **every order** that customer places, not just the first one. This creates a powerful stream of passive, recurring income.

### 3. Team Commissions (Optional)
For those looking to build a larger business, Touchstone offers tiered commissions on team volume. If you introduce other Ambassadors to the program, you can earn a percentage of their sales as well. This "direct-sales" component is optional but highly lucrative for those with leadership skills.
