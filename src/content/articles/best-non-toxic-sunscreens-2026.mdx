Conventional sunscreen is full of questionable chemicals. Oxybenzone, octinoxate, homosalate — ingredients that absorb into your bloodstream and may disrupt hormones.

The good news? Clean mineral sunscreens have come a long way. Today's non-toxic options go on smoothly, leave minimal white cast, and actually protect your skin with zinc oxide — a mineral that sits on top of your skin and reflects UV rays.

We rounded up 12 of the best non-toxic and low-tox sunscreens for the whole family, from budget-friendly picks to tallow-based formulas that double as moisturizer.

## What to Look For in a Non-Toxic Sunscreen

- **Active Ingredient:** Non-nano zinc oxide (not chemical filters)
- **SPF:** 30-50 for daily use, 50+ for extended outdoor time
- **Minimal Ingredients:** The shorter the list, the better
- **Reef Safe:** Free of oxybenzone and octinoxate
- **No Fragrance (optional):** Better for sensitive skin
