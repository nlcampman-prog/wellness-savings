### FAQ

**Q: Can I use collagen as my only protein source?**
A: No. While collagen is amazing for joints and skin, it is not a "complete" protein for muscle building as it lacks certain essential amino acids like tryptophan. It should be used *in addition* to a complete protein like Equip’s Prime Protein.

**Q: Is beef protein better than whey?**
A: For many people, yes. Beef isolate protein is generally easier to digest and doesn't cause the bloating or skin breakouts that some people experience with dairy-based whey.

**Q: How much protein do I actually need?**
A: A general rule of thumb for active individuals is roughly 0.8g to 1g of protein per pound of target body weight.
