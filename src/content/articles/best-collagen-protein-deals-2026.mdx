## FAQ: Protein & Collagen
### Can I mix protein and collagen?
Yes! Many people add a scoop of collagen to their morning protein shake. Collagen provides the amino acids for connective tissue, while protein isolate provides the leucine needed for muscle protein synthesis.
### When is the best time to take protein?
While the "30-minute anabolic window" is largely a myth, getting 20-30g of protein within 2 hours of your workout is ideal for recovery.
### How much protein do I actually need?
A good rule of thumb for 2026 is **0.8g to 1g of protein per pound of ideal body weight**, especially if you are active or looking to maintain muscle mass while losing fat.
