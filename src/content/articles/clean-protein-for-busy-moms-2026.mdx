## FAQ: Protein for Moms

### Is beef protein better than whey?
For many women, yes. Whey is a common allergen and can cause significant bloating and acne. Beef isolate (like Equip) provides the same high-quality amino acids without the inflammatory side effects of dairy.

### Can I take Perfect Amino while breastfeeding?
Perfect Amino is just a concentrated form of the amino acids found in your food. However, as with any supplement, you should always consult your healthcare provider or lactation consultant before starting.

### How do I make the Equip protein taste the best?
