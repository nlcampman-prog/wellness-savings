## The Hazards of Conventional Beauty Products

Before looking at our top-rated natural alternatives, it is crucial to understand what ingredients you should actively purge from your beauty cabinet:

1.  **Synthetic Sulfates (SLS/SLES):** Harsh chemical detergents used to create foam in shampoos and body washes. They strip the scalp and skin of their natural, protective oils, leading to chronic dryness, eczema, and hair loss.
2.  **Parabens & Phthalates:** Commonly used as cheap preservatives and fragrance stabilizers. These compounds are known **endocrine disruptors**, meaning they can mimic estrogen and disrupt hormonal cycles, particularly in women.
3.  **Aluminum Salts & Baking Soda:** Found in traditional deodorants. Aluminum plugs sweat glands, while abrasive baking soda disrupts underarm pH, causing painful red rashes and skin peeling.
