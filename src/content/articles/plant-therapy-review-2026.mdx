## The Plant Therapy Difference: Quality and Safety

What sets Plant Therapy apart in a crowded market? It comes down to three key pillars:

### 1. Radical Transparency (GC/MS Testing)
Every bottle of Plant Therapy essential oil has a unique batch code. You can enter this code on their website to see the Gas Chromatography/Mass Spectrometry (GC/MS) report, which proves the oil is 100% pure and free from contaminants or synthetics.

### 2. The KidSafe® Line
Safety is paramount when using essential oils around children. Plant Therapy’s **KidSafe® line** was developed by world-renowned essential oil safety expert Robert Tisserand. These blends are specifically formulated to be safe for children aged 2–10, taking the guesswork out of aromatherapy for parents.

### 3. Sustainability and Ethics
Plant Therapy is committed to ethical sourcing and sustainable practices. They work closely with farmers around the world to ensure that their products are sourced responsibly and that the workers are treated fairly.

## FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

### Are Plant Therapy oils "therapeutic grade"?
The term "therapeutic grade" is actually a marketing term rather than a regulated industry standard. However, Plant Therapy oils are 100% pure and meet all the criteria typically associated with that designation, backed by GC/MS testing.

### How do I know which oils are safe for my kids?
Look for the **KidSafe® logo** on the bottle or product page. These oils have been specifically vetted for safety when used as directed on children.

### Can I ingest Plant Therapy essential oils?
Plant Therapy does not generally recommend the internal use of essential oils unless under the supervision of a clinical aromatherapist or healthcare professional, as oils are highly concentrated.
