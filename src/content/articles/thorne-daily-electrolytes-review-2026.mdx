## The Importance of Cellular Hydration

Proper hydration isn't just about quenching thirst. It’s about **osmotic balance**—the process by which your cells move nutrients in and waste products out. For this to happen efficiently, your body needs electrolytes like sodium, potassium, and magnesium.

When your electrolyte levels are imbalanced, you may experience:
*   Brain fog and "afternoon slumps."
*   Persistent fatigue despite sleeping well.
*   Headaches and muscle tension.
*   Dry skin and poor digestion.

Thorne Daily Electrolytes provides these minerals in highly absorbable forms, helping you maintain energy and clarity from the inside out.
