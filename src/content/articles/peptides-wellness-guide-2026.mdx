# Peptides for Wellness 2026: The Complete Guide

Peptides have become one of the hottest topics in health and wellness in 2026 — and for good reason. From accelerated recovery to cognitive enhancement, these short chains of amino acids are changing how we approach wellness at the cellular level.

## What Are Peptides?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids — the building blocks of proteins. Unlike full proteins, peptides are small enough to be absorbed rapidly by the body, making them highly effective for targeted therapeutic effects.

### How They Work

Peptides act as signaling molecules, telling your body to perform specific functions:

- **Collagen peptides** — signal skin and joint repair
- **BPC-157** — accelerates injury recovery
- **GHK-Cu** — supports skin regeneration and hair growth
- **MOTS-c** — enhances metabolic function

## Types of Wellness Peptides

### Recovery & Injury Healing
BPC-157 and TB-500 are the most researched peptides for injury recovery, reducing inflammation, and accelerating tissue repair.

### Skin & Beauty
GHK-Cu (copper peptide) is widely used for collagen production, reducing fine lines, and improving skin elasticity. It's become a key ingredient in medical-grade skincare.

### Metabolic Health
MOTS-c and AOD-9604 support fat metabolism and glucose regulation, making them popular for metabolic optimization.

### Cognitive Function
Semax and Cerebrolysin are nootropic peptides that support focus, memory, and neuroprotection.
