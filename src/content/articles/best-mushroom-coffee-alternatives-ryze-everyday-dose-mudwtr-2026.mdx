## The Mushroom Coffee Boom: Why Everyone Is Switching in 2026

Before diving into the price comparison, it is worth understanding what is driving the mushroom coffee trend — because the "why" directly affects which brand makes sense for you.

Traditional coffee works by blocking adenosine receptors in your brain, which prevents you from feeling tired. The problem? It also spikes cortisol (your stress hormone), irritates the stomach lining with high acidity, and often leads to a mid-morning energy crash when the caffeine wears off.

Mushroom coffee works differently. By combining a moderate dose of caffeine — typically 40–50mg per serving, about half the amount in a regular cup — with functional mushrooms like Lion's Mane, Chaga, Cordyceps, and Reishi, these blends aim to provide:

- **Sustained, crash-free energy:** The lower caffeine paired with adaptogenic mushrooms smooths out the energy curve.
- **Cognitive focus:** Lion's Mane is extensively researched for stimulating Nerve Growth Factor (NGF), which supports brain cell health and mental clarity.
- **Gut-friendliness:** Many mushroom coffee blends are less acidic than traditional coffee and include prebiotic fibers that support digestive health.
- **Immune support:** Chaga and Reishi are packed with beta-glucans and antioxidants that modulate the immune system.

The three brands in this comparison — Ryze, Everyday Dose, and MUD/WTR — all promise these benefits. But their formulas, pricing, and overall value differ significantly.
