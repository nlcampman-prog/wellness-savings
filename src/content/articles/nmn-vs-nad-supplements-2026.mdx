## The Science: Why You Cannot Just Swallow NAD+

To understand the difference between NMN and NAD+, we must look at how our cells absorb nutrients.

*   **NAD+ is Too Big:** The physical NAD+ molecule is very large and highly complex. Because of its size, raw oral NAD+ supplements cannot successfully cross the cell membrane. If you swallow a capsule of pure NAD+, your digestive tract and liver will break it down before it ever reaches your cells.
*   **The Power of Precursors:** To raise your cellular NAD+ levels, you must feed your body **precursors**—smaller, highly bioavailable building blocks that *can* easily cross the cell membrane. Once inside the cell, these precursors are instantly converted into active NAD+.
*   **NMN (Nicotinamide Mononucleotide):** NMN is the direct, immediate precursor to NAD+. It is absorbed rapidly by specialized transporters in the gut, entering the bloodstream within minutes. Once inside the cells, it requires only one simple chemical step to become active, life-giving NAD+.
