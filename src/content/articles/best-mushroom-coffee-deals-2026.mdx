## Why Switch to Mushroom Coffee?
Standard coffee provides a spike in cortisol and a temporary block of adenosine (the molecule that makes you feel tired). Once the caffeine wears off, the adenosine floods back, leading to the dreaded "crash."

Mushroom coffee typically uses a base of organic instant coffee or cacao blended with adaptogenic mushrooms like:
- **Lion’s Mane:** For cognitive function and "brain fog" reduction.
- **Chaga:** For immune support and antioxidants.
- **Reishi:** For stress management and better sleep quality.
- **Cordyceps:** For natural energy and athletic performance.

## FAQ: What You Need to Know Before Buying
### Does mushroom coffee taste like mushrooms?
Surprisingly, no. Functional mushrooms like Lion's Mane and Chaga have an earthy, bitter profile that blends perfectly with coffee or cacao. You won't feel like you're drinking "fungus soup."
### Is it safe for pregnancy/nursing?
Most mushroom coffees are low-caffeine and use natural ingredients, but always consult your doctor. Brands like **Needed** offer specific nutritional support for this stage of life.
### Can I add creamer?
Absolutely. Mushroom coffee pairs beautifully with oat milk, almond milk, or traditional dairy. Ryze even offers a coconut milk-based creamer that is delicious.
