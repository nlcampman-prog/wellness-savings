## Deodorant vs. Antiperspirant: Understanding the Difference

Before reviewing the brands, it is highly crucial to understand what natural deodorants actually do:

*   **Antiperspirants (Use Aluminum):** Use aluminum-based compounds to temporarily plug your sweat pores, physically preventing your body from perspiring.
*   **Natural Deodorants (Aluminum-Free):** Do *not* stop you from sweating (sweating is a natural, essential process your body uses to regulate temperature and detoxify). Instead, they target the **bacteria** that live on your skin.
*   **The Cause of Odor:** Sweat itself is actually completely odorless. Body odor is only created when the proteins and fats in your sweat are consumed and metabolized by the natural bacteria residing on your skin. Natural deodorants use safe, botanical, and pH-balancing ingredients to prevent this bacterial metabolism from occurring.
