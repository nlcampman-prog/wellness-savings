## What is GLP-1 and Why Does It Matter?

GLP-1 is often called the "satiety hormone." When you eat, your gut releases GLP-1, which does three main things:
1.  **Signals Fullness:** It tells your brain you've had enough to eat.
2.  **Slows Gastric Emptying:** It keeps food in your stomach longer, prolonging that "full" feeling.
3.  **Insulin Regulation:** It helps your pancreas release the right amount of insulin to manage blood sugar.

Natural alternatives don't replace the hormone; they provide the body with the building blocks or triggers to produce and maintain its own GLP-1 levels more effectively.
