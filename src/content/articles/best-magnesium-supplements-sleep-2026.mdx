## FAQ: Magnesium for Sleep

### What is the best form of magnesium for sleep?
**Magnesium Bisglycinate** (or Glycinate) is widely considered the best form for sleep and anxiety. The magnesium is bound to glycine, an amino acid that has its own calming effects on the brain.

### When should I take magnesium?
For sleep optimization, it is best to take your magnesium **30-60 minutes before bed**. If you are using a powder like Needed or Beam, this allows the nutrients to enter your system just as your body is preparing for its natural sleep cycle.

### Can I take magnesium every day?
Yes, magnesium is generally safe for daily use. However, always consult with your healthcare provider, especially if you have kidney issues or are on medication.
