## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is sprouted pea protein better than regular pea protein?
Yes, absolutely! Sprouting (or germinating) the yellow peas before processing them is highly beneficial. The sprouting process actively breaks down **phytic acid and lectins**—naturally occurring plant compounds that bind to minerals and restrict their absorption in the gut. Sprouted pea protein is significantly more bioavailable, allowing your body to absorb iron, zinc, and amino acids far more efficiently while being much gentler on digestion.
