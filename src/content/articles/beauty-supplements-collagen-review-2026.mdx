## What Are Beauty Supplements?

Beauty supplements are ingestible products designed to support skin, hair, and nail health from the inside out. The most popular categories in 2026 include:

- **Collagen peptides** — support skin elasticity and hydration
- **"Glow" blends** — vitamins, antioxidants, and hyaluronic acid for radiance
- **Biotin complexes** — hair and nail growth support
- **Marine collagen** — Type I collagen specifically for skin

## Why They're Trending in 2026

Three factors are driving the beauty supplement boom:

1. **The "Skinification" of wellness** — people want to treat skin health like overall health
2. **Convenience** — one scoop vs. a 10-step skincare routine
3. **Celebrity endorsements** — everyone from supermodels to actors are promoting ingestible beauty
