## What to Look for in a Greens Powder in 2026

To ensure you are getting premium, clinical-grade nutrition rather than cheap grass fillers, check the label for these three standards:

1.  **Cold-Processed Organic Ingredients:** Heat destroys delicate enzymes and vitamins. Premium greens are dried and ground using low-temperature methods to preserve nutritional integrity.
2.  **No Proproprietary Blends (Transparency):** Cheap brands hide low dosages under "proprietary blends" so you don't know how much of each ingredient is actually present. Look for transparent labeling that lists exact milligram dosages.
3.  **Probiotics & Digestive Support:** High-quality greens include digestive enzymes (like amylase, lipase, and cellulase) and stable probiotic strains (like *Lactobacillus acidophilus*) to maximize absorption and eliminate bloating.
