## Tips for a Better Night's Rest in 2026

1.  **Cool Your Room:** Your body temperature needs to drop to initiate sleep. Aim for 65–68°F.
2.  **The 3-2-1 Rule:** No food 3 hours before bed, no work 2 hours before, and no screens 1 hour before.
3.  **Morning Sunlight:** Get 10 minutes of direct sunlight as soon as you wake up to "set" your internal clock.
