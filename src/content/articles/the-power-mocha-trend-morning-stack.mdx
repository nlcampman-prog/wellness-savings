## 4. How to Make the Viral TikTok "Power Mocha" (Step-by-Step)

Making this viral morning stack is incredibly simple and takes less than two minutes. The secret is using a handheld milk frother to emulsify the healthy fats and protein fibers for a thick, luxurious, coffeehouse-style texture.

### Ingredients:
*   1 tablespoon **Ryze Mushroom Coffee**
*   1 scoop Chocolate **Equip Foods Prime Protein**
*   8-10 ounces hot water (around 185°F - not boiling, to protect the delicate adaptogens)
*   2-3 ounces warm almond milk, coconut milk, or grass-fed raw milk
*   *Optional:* A pinch of organic cinnamon or a splash of pure vanilla extract

### Instructions:
1.  **Whisk the Base:** Add 1 tablespoon of Ryze Mushroom Coffee to a large mug and pour in 8-10 ounces of hot water. Use a handheld frother to whisk for 10 seconds until a light froth forms.
2.  **Add the Fuel:** Add 1 full scoop of Chocolate Equip Prime Protein directly into the hot coffee. 
3.  **Froth to Perfection:** Submerge your handheld milk frother and blend for 15-20 seconds. The grass-fed beef isolate will emulsify beautifully with the coffee and hot water, turning into a rich, thick, creamy chocolate mocha base.
4.  **Top it Off:** Pour in your favorite warm warm milk or creamer, and top with a sprinkle of cinnamon. Drink, focus, and conquer your day!

*Note: You can easily make this an **Iced Power Mocha** by blending the ingredients with a splash of warm water first to dissolve the protein, adding cold milk, and pouring it over a large glass of ice!*
