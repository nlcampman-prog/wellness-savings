Thrive Market has become one of the most popular online marketplaces for organic, non-GMO, and sustainable groceries. But is the membership fee worth it? We tested Thrive Market for 3 months to find out.

## What Is Thrive Market?

Thrive Market is a membership-based online retailer offering 6,000+ products at wholesale prices — from organic groceries and supplements to clean beauty and eco-friendly home goods.

Think of it as **Costco for healthy living**, but online and delivered to your door.

## How the Membership Works

- **Annual membership:** ~$5/month ($60/year)
- **Wholesale pricing:** 25-50% off retail prices on most items
- **Free shipping** on orders over $49
- **Thrive Gives:** Every paid membership funds a free membership for a low-income family
