## Sourcing & Ingredient Quality: Why Fruiting Bodies Matter

When purchasing any mushroom supplement, the single most important question you must ask is: **"Are you using the fruiting body or the mycelium?"**

Unfortunately, the vast majority of cheap, commercial mushroom products on the market are highly adulterated. 

These brands grow **mycelium** (the microscopic root system of the mushroom) inside plastic bags filled with sterile grains (like oats, rice, or barley). 

Because the root system cannot be separated from the grain, the entire mass is ground up into a powder. The result? A product that is up to **70% starch and filler** (grain) with very little active mushroom content.
