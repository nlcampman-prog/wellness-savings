### 1. Plant-Based Vitamin D Cream

Conventional vitamin D supplements come in two forms: synthetic D2 (ergocalciferol, derived from irradiated yeast) or D3 from lanolin (sheep's wool grease). Earthley takes a completely different approach: their **Vitamin D Cream** delivers vitamin D through the skin using a plant-based carrier.

**Why this matters:** Vitamin D is a fat-soluble vitamin, meaning it is absorbed through lipid membranes. Topical application — especially when combined with a nourishing base of coconut oil, shea butter, and olive oil — allows for slow, steady absorption that mimics how your body would naturally synthesize vitamin D from sunlight. For people who struggle with swallowing pills, have digestive absorption issues, or want to avoid animal-derived lanolin, this is an elegant solution.

**Key features:**
- Plant-based vitamin D3 from lichen (vegan-friendly)
- Absorbed through the skin, bypassing the digestive system
- Combined with moisturizing oils — doubles as a daily body cream
- No synthetic preservatives or fragrances

**Best for:** Anyone with low vitamin D who dislikes pills, vegans, and those with gut absorption challenges.

### 2. Natural Magnesium Lotion

Magnesium deficiency is one of the most widespread nutrient gaps — particularly among women who exercise, experience high stress, or are postpartum. Symptoms like muscle cramps, restless legs, poor sleep, anxiety, and hormonal migraines are frequently linked to low magnesium levels.

Earthley's **Magnesium Lotion** delivers bioavailable magnesium chloride through the skin — an approach known as transdermal magnesium therapy. This method has several advantages over oral magnesium supplements:

- **No digestive distress:** Many oral magnesium forms (especially magnesium oxide and citrate) cause bloating, cramping, and diarrhea at effective doses. Transdermal absorption completely avoids this.
- **Targeted relief:** You can apply the lotion directly to sore muscles, cramping calves, or tight shoulders for localized relief.
- **Bedtime-friendly:** Applied before bed, magnesium lotion supports relaxation and deeper sleep through both muscle relaxation and nervous system calming.

**Key features:**
- Magnesium chloride sourced from ancient seabeds
- Combined with soothing coconut oil and shea butter
- Absorbs quickly, non-greasy finish
- Unscented or lightly scented with essential oils

**Best for:** Anyone with magnesium deficiency symptoms, poor sleep, muscle cramps, or sensitivity to oral magnesium.
