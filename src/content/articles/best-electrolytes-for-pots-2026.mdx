## The POTS Hydration Challenge: Sodium Without the "Junk"

The clinical recommendation for POTS often involves consuming 3,000mg to 10,000mg of sodium per day. To reach these levels using standard electrolyte packets, you would have to consume a staggering amount of sugar or stevia, which can lead to:
- **Insulin spikes and crashes** (mimicking POTS symptoms like dizziness).
- **Gut inflammation** from sugar alcohols or artificial sweeteners.
- **"Flavor fatigue"** from drinking sweet beverages all day long.
