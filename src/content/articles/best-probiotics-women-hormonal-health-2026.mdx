## FAQ: Probiotics and Hormones

### What is the "Estrobolome"?
The **Estrobolome** is the collection of bacteria in your gut that helps regulate estrogen levels. If these bacteria are imbalanced, you may reabsorb estrogen that should have been eliminated, leading to "estrogen dominance."

### Can probiotics help with anxiety?
Yes. The **gut-brain axis** is a two-way street. Recent 2026 studies have shown that certain probiotic strains (often called "psychobiotics") can reduce cortisol levels and improve mood by supporting neurotransmitter production in the gut.

### How long does it take to see results?
While some women notice a reduction in bloating within days, the hormonal benefits of a probiotic typically take **2-3 months** of consistent use as the microbiome shifts and stabilizes.
