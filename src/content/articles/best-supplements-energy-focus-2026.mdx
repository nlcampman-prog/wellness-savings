### How long until I see results?
Stimulants like caffeine work in 20 minutes, but nutritional supplements like Thorne B-Complex or Needed typically take **2-4 weeks** to build up in your system and provide noticeable "baseline" energy.

While not a formal medical diagnosis, "HPA-axis dysfunction" (the medical term) is a well-documented result of chronic stress. Supplements like the adaptogens found in AG1 are designed specifically to support this system.
